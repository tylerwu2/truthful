Local Man Discovers That Water Is Actually Wet

In a groundbreaking scientific discovery, researchers at the Institute for the Obvious have confirmed that water is, in fact, wet.

"This changes everything we thought we knew about moisture," said Dr. Captain Obvious, lead researcher on the project.

The study, which took three years and cost $50 million in taxpayer dollars, involved carefully observing water in various situations.

Critics of the research argue that this was already common knowledge, but scientists maintain that empirical evidence is important.
