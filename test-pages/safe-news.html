Market Update: Tech Stocks Rise

NEW YORK (Reuters) - Technology stocks advanced on Wednesday as investors welcomed stronger-than-expected earnings from major companies.

Apple Inc (AAPL.O) shares rose 2.3% after the company reported quarterly revenue of $119.4 billion, beating analyst expectations.

Microsoft Corp (MSFT.O) gained 1.8% following its cloud computing segment growth announcement.

Analysts attributed the gains to solid economic data and positive guidance from tech leaders.

The Nasdaq Composite Index closed up 1.2% for the day.
